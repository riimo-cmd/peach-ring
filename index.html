Want to join?

Send an email to kurohaato@yahoo.com with the subject line "Peach Ring" and the following information:

  • Site name
  • Link to the page that displays the content
  • Short discription of what food related content you host on your site

You will recive a reply within a week to let you know if you have been accepted.

Rules:

  • Sites that host 18+ content or encourage eating disorders will not be allowed in the Peach Ring.

  • Sites must have their food content accessable through a link somewhere on their site.
  • Content can be in any format.
  • Content must be hosted by the site. Simply linking to your allrecipies does not count. Exceptions for external embeds will be made on a case-by-case basis.
  • Sites that host content relating to alchohol will be allowed into the webring on a case-by-case basis.

If your site URL stops working, the widget is removed from your site, or if you start hosting content that violates the rules of the webring, you will be emailed via the address you orignally used to apply. If there is no reply for a month, your site will be removed from the webring.
